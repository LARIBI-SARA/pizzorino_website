Our pizzas

Margherita

Roll out the pizza dough, spread a layer of tomato sauce, top it with slices of fresh mozzarella cheese, and scatter some torn fresh basil leaves over the pizza. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper, then bake in a preheated oven at 220°C (425°F) for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. Enjoy your delicious Pizza Margherita! 🍕🌿

Meat Pizza

Roll out the pizza dough, spread a layer of tomato sauce, then top it with a generous amount of cooked and seasoned ground meat, shredded mozzarella cheese, and your favorite meat toppings. Bake in a preheated oven at 220°C (425°F) for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Enjoy your delicious Meat Lovers' Pizza! 🍕🍖

Vegetarian Pizza

Roll out the pizza dough, spread a layer of tomato sauce, then top it with a colorful array of sliced bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, black olives, and any other favorite vegetable toppings, followed by a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese. Bake in a preheated oven at 220°C (425°F) for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Sprinkle with fresh basil leaves or red pepper flakes if desired. Enjoy your delicious Vegetarian Pizza! 🍕🥦🌽
